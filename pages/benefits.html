Benefits of registering with the Pension Angel

Once registered, use our tools to easily see:

  • How each of your pension funds' performance compares against other pensions funds - including the best and worst.
  • Assessments of each of your funds' growth, and if changing the fund could be worthwhile.
  • Up to ten better-performing alternatives for any poor-performing pension fund of your own.
  • Risk warnings that tell you when it is safest to put new pension contributions into low-risk funds.
  • Risk warnings that tell you when existing equity fund holdings should be moved to safer funds.

Everything you need to talk knowledgeably with your Independent Financial Advisor and ensure your pension funds are growing in line with the best funds.

And what you can do if they aren't!

Whether registered or not, all users are welcome to read our:

  • Tips for managing pension funds
  • Current balance of gains and losses across all pension funds
  • Historical balance of gains and losses across all pension funds
